About Kanha Agro Foods

Established in 1972, founded by Late Shri Ram Kumar Bansal, Jai Mata Dal Mill has been a trusted name in the Indian pulses industry for over five decades. Renowned for its commitment to purity, quality, and nutrition, the company has earned the confidence of households across India. In 2018, Mr. Amit Bansal carried this legacy forward by launching Kanha Agro Foods, expanding the brand's reach with a modern vision. Combining traditional expertise with advanced processing technology, the company continues to deliver chemical free, Hygienic and premium-quality food products. Every product is carefully crafted to ensure authentic taste, freshness, and superior quality.
